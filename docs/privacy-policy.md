# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 4, 2026
Effective date: March 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Homely ("Homely", "we", "us", "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects personal information when you use:

- the Homely mobile application;

By using the Services, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, information collected automatically, and information from service providers.

### 1.1 Information you provide

- Images and media you upload (for example, room photos and masks).
- Prompts or text you enter in the app.
- Output content generated for you.
- Messages you send to support.

### 1.2 Information collected automatically

- Device and app information (device type, OS version, app version, language, region).
- Unique app identifiers (for example, guest/account identifiers used for credits and processing).
- Basic usage and diagnostic data (feature usage, errors, crash diagnostics, performance events).

### 1.3 Purchase and billing information

- Purchases are processed by Apple App Store / Google Play and our subscription infrastructure provider.
- We receive transaction metadata (for example: product ID, subscription status, expiry, and entitlement state).
- We do not receive or store full payment card numbers.
